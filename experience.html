research

building blocks of cognition lab

oct 2024 - present

i work under professor silvia bunge on the RelDom project’s 3T and 7T fMRI studies. our project investigates the role of the rostrolateral prefrontal cortex (rLPFC) in relational reasoning and the domain-sensitivity of working memory. i manage the behavioral tasks of the fMRI sessions, such as administering screening and demographics surveys, conducting practice task runs with the participant, and running stimuli scans on PsychoPy. these scans consist of multivariate and functional connectivity analyses to test how the rLPFC considers relations between mental representations. additionally, i assist with participant recruitment and scheduling.

golden bear sleep and mood research clinic

sep 2024 - present

under professor allison harvey’s NIA study, i am a clinical assessor as part of the assessment team. our study examines the efficacy of a novel sleep therapy on elderly individuals experiencing sleep difficulties. my role includes screening participants for study eligibility, administering clinical questionnaires and assessments (e.g. MoCA, PROMIS-SD, C-SSRS, BDQ, WHODAS), and inputting data into qualtrics and excel.
