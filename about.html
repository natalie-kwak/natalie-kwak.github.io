hi! i’m natalie, a first-year at uc berkeley studying psychology and economics. i currently work as an undergraduate research assistant in two labs here at cal. at the building blocks of cognition lab, i help to administer 3T and 7T f/MRI scans that explore the role of the prefrontal cortex in relational reasoning and working memory. at the golden bear sleep and mood research clinic, i’m a clinical assessor, screening and surveying aging individuals who endorse sleep difficulties.

my current interests/hobbies include building this website (first time coding!), cooking, collecting postcards, and visiting berkeley bowl west. please don’t hesitate to reach out!
